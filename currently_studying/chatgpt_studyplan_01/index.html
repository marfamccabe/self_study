My love for matcha

My go-to order from my favorite coffee shop is a matcha latte. Coffee has its moments, but a bright, vibrant matcha always hits the spot. Hot or iced, matcha lattes are perfect any time of day. I personally love one with oat milk and just a touch of vanilla or lavender syrup.

If you've never had a matcha latte, it's worth the hype. Some people don't like the grassy taste, but that just means it's high quality tea! Matcha lattes have just enough caffiene to get you focused, plus it has L-theanine, which helps with acheiving a more relaxed mood. Get the focus you need without the jitters and enjoy a beautiful, delicious matcha soon!
