My love for matcha

My go-to order from my favorite coffee shop is a matcha latte. Coffee has its moments, but a bright, vibrant matcha always hits the spot. Hot or iced, matcha lattes are perfect any time of day. I personally love one with oat milk and just a touch of vanilla or lavender syrup.

If you've never had a matcha latte, it's worth the hype. Some people don't like the grassy taste, but that just means it's high quality tea! Some benefits of drinking matcha are:

  • High in antioxidants, which support the immune system
  • Enhances mood & focus without the jitters
  • Helps detoxify the body
